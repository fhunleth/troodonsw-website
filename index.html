Troodon Software provides software and systems development and consulting expertise for embedded and real-time systems in IoT, consumer electronics, networking, mobile, and government. Capabilities include:

  • Elixir and Erlang-based embedded systems design and development
  • Nerves and Buildroot porting, configuration, and development
  • Embedded firmware updates and distribution using fwup
  • Embedded Linux device driver, kernel, and Qt-based application development
  • Camera, video, and image processing path design, optimization, and tuning
  • Custom hardware integration and efficient software API design
  • Embedded system architecture and design
  • HDMI, USB, and WiFi integration, test, and certification
  • TI Davinci, OMAP, and AM335x, Sigma Designs, many other ARM, MIPS, and Intel processors
  • NVIDIA and AMD GPU-based development

If your application is taking a turn towards custom hardware and embedded development, let us know. We're not your typical embedded developers. We strive for technical simplicity in solutions, but that doesn't mean that everything is developed in C and bare metal (but we do that too). We'll work with you and your team so that everyone is comfortable with the embedded side. Embedded Linux doesn't need to be overwhelming, complicated, and foreign. Everyone, no matter what their background, should be able to contribute and maintain the embedded codebase. It may just take some help to get started and get past a few hard pieces.
